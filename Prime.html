Amazon Prime Lounge

The final design was an evolution of real world testing. It allows people to enjoy watching and talking about movies together, wherever they are. It does this by allowing people to co-watch anything on Amazon Prime and creates group conversations for everyone involved.

Brief

Our brief was to improve the social aspect of Amazon Prime Instant Video. The aim was to recall the shared social experience of TV in the past to the social media heavy instant video accessible future.

Competitive Analysis

We saw that there were many streaming services but very few had achieved social success however sites such as Twitch streaming gaming live had managed this with 45million users and is one of the few services promoting watching content with people live this seemed key to their success.

Surveys

Our Typeform survey informed us people watch in a variety of ways but prefered to use larger desktop screens or TVs for viewing and mobile for talking about what they watched.

User Interviews

We interviewed a number of users to gain qualitative information and they showed some key patterns, importantly for us that most of them loved to share their viewing experiences and that this was most commonly done soon after or sometimes during watching.

Personas & Scenarios

We created personas and for them scenarios to help better understand our users and their situations. This informed us currently trying to sync viewing times with friends is a difficult experience when streaming.

Features

Two main features were chosen. To be able to set up films that could be watched at a scheduled time on everyones devices (co-watching) and a chatroom running during and after for groups to share their experiences. We called this sycned feature "Prime Lounge" to echo the shared living room experience of the past.

Sketching

We conducted a design studio session initially to rapidly brainstorm different design solutions which we then developed into our chosen solution.

User Testing

Testing was conducted at a local cinema lounge to help capture our target audience for relevant testing. They found the app easy to use with just a few tweeks needed and told us they loved the idea and would even have liked it for personal videos as well as mainstream film and TV content.
