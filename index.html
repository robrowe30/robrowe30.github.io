About Me

I am new to the design industry but have had an interest in it for a long time. I love watching how with time technology seems to always offer more yet when designed well is increasingly intuitive in its use. Click about at the top to learn more about me.

Design

I believe we design to both help people accomplish what they set out to do and to make sure the experience of doing this is as pleasurable as possible. I believe this can be achieved by reducing clutter and friction to concentrate on what people really require from a product.

My Work

My time in the industry has begun as a student at General Assembly completing various projects over a 10 week intensive UX course. During this time I have learned many design skills demonstrated within projects on this site and now look to build upon them.
